The Irish Car Bomb
Description
You might have seen this drink in moves and other media, and for good reason: the Irish Car Bomb/Irish Slammer is a fun, flavor-packed drink that's easy to make. This drink is named after a dark time in Irish history known as the Troubles, when car bombings were common. Impress your car-loving friends with this historical fact when you pour them an Irish Car Bomb.
Ingredients
  • ½ ounce Irish whiskey
  • ½ ounce Baileys Irish cream
  • A cold glass of Guinness beer
Mixing Steps
  1. Pour the Baileys and whiskey into a shot glass
  2. Promptly drop it into the Guinness
  3. Drink up before the cream curdles
