The Silver Mercedes
Description
Named after the high-class Mercedes Benz brand car enthusiasts adore, the Silver Mercedes adds a hint of luxury to the classic vodka cran.
Ingredients
  • 2 ounces of vodka
  • 1 ounce of cranberry juice
  • Champagne floater
Mixing Steps
  1. Chill the vodka and cranberry juice
  2. Pour into a glass
  3. Stir with ice
  4. Top it off with as much champagne as you want
