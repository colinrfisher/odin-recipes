The Bentley Cocktail
Description
The Bentley cocktail pays tribute to the British auto manufacturer with a simple yet elegant drink that people have enjoyed since the early days of the famous Savoy Hotel in London.If the car enthusiast you know is looking for a strong and satisfying drink to mull over, the Bentley cocktail is the choice for them.
Ingredients
  • 1½ ounces of Calvados
  • 1½ ounces of Dubonnet Rouge
  • Three dashes of orange bitters
Mixing Steps
  1. Gently stir the ingredients
  2. Pour into a chilled glass
  3. Enjoy
